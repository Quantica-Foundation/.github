## Mission:

The Quantica Foundation exists to:

Design and evolve the Quantica programming language

Build open, educational resources around the language

Encourage learning through examples, experimentation, and creation.

Support a growing ecosystem of tools, libraries, and projects

Nurture a community that is built on curiosity, clarity, and rigorous thinking.

This is not about hype.

It is about usable ideas.

---

## Philosophy:

Learning by building beats learning by reading

Small, working programs teach more than abstract descriptions

Simplicity and clarity are more important than cleverness.

Innovation should remain understandable.

Quantum computing should be approachable, not obtuse.

Quantica is designed to be explored, not memorized.

---

## Core Areas:

The foundation maintains and supports:

**Quantica Language:**

The core language, compiler, runtime and standard library.

**Quantica Projects Hub:**

A curated collection of beginner-friendly mini projects demonstrating what can be built with Quantica.

**Learning Resources:**

Examples, documentation, tutorials and experimental playing around.

Over time, this may expand to tooling, research experiments, and community-driven extensions.

## Direction:

Quantica is experimental, yet intentional. We believe in focused, incremental evolution, real-world applicability, and organic ecosystem growth through usage, not marketing. If you can read a Quantica program, you should feel confident changing it. If you can change it, you can build new.
